What the dashboard measures

Influence

Uses citation volume, h-index, and long-run publication footprint.

Paper quality

Emphasizes field-normalized citation percentile, top-10% share, and core venues.

Publication cadence

Tracks recent output, recency of publications, and year-by-year research tempo.

Collaboration network

Maps recurring collaborators, notable senior collaborators, and recent coauthor structure.

Identity evidence

Marks a coauthor as student or junior only when an external record states that career stage explicitly.

Important limitation

Student status, advising quality, and hidden lab structure cannot be proven automatically unless explicit public evidence exists. The dashboard therefore prefers conservative labels over speculative claims.
