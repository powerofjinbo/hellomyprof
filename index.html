What the report measures

Influence

Uses citation volume, h-index, and long-run publication footprint.

Paper quality

Emphasizes field-normalized citation percentile, top-10% share, and core venues.

Publication cadence

Tracks recent output, recency of publications, and year-by-year research tempo.

Collaboration network

Maps recurring collaborators, notable senior collaborators, and recent coauthor structure.

Evidence integrity

Separates structured publication evidence from supporting website evidence and marks the boundary explicitly.

Important limitation

Student status, advising quality, and hidden lab structure cannot be proven automatically unless explicit public evidence exists. The report therefore prefers conservative signals over speculative claims.
